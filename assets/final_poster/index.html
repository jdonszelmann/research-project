Multi-agent pathfinding

Multi agent pathfinding (MAPF) is finding collision-free paths for multiple agents.

Matching in MAPF

Grouping agents in MAPF into teams gives MAPFM, MAPF with matching. Agents travel to a goal of their team. An assignment of agents to goals is called a matching.
